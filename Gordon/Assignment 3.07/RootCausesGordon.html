Root Causes #1

People are less likely to engage to current Astronomy teachings (Lectures). Other learning methods should be considered when approaching the learning of astronomy.

Evidence#1

In Science education Issues and Developments by Calvin L Petroselli, Michael C LoPresto of Henry Ford Community College states that "Since current research shows that most students learn very little from lectures, other approaches need to be employed."

Explanation#1

This shows the lack of educational resources that are implimented to Astronomy. Not all students learn and engage well in a lecture style class. Other instruction tailored toward different learning styles should be included, specifically in the visual learning department.

Evidence#2

Since astronomy isn't a hands on study of science, the current atempts at instructing interested learning in astronomy are difficult. "A study in 2022 showed that there were similar dificulties to students understanding key concepts in Astronomy."

Explanation#2

Astronomy faces a huge challenge when it comes to education. The limitations that come from being a less hands on science brings into account less active learning. Traditional learning can't apply the necessary engagement and interest to all students who take an introductory course to Astronomy.
